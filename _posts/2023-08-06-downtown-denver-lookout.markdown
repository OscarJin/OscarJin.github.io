Last time when I [visited Denver][citywalk-denver], I was deeply impressed by the photographic spot -- Downtown Denver Lookout on Zuni Street. I bet the scenery there would be much more beautiful at dusk. So weeks later, I paid a second visit and it turned out I was right. The skyline of Denver at the blue moment was just so splendid.

![](DenverLookout.jpg)
_Denver Skyline at Blue Moment_

In the video, I documented the process of downtown Denver changing from the golden hour of sunset to the blue moment after sunset, with a little bit of ChatGPT-generated copywriting and La La Land's most famous City of Stars.
